History Settings

Keep History for Day(s)
? History is cleared on startup and for every hour the browser is open. Does a manual cleanup when turning on for the first time.

Log Total Number Of History Deleted
? Counts the number of history deleted during this session and in total. This is shown in the Welcome Screen.

Show Number of Visits In Browser Icon
? Shows how many history entries that domain has in your history. Numbers will fluctuate if you have have older history automatically deleted.
